# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free, and inclusive environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Focus feedback on ideas and code, not people.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Repeated disruptive or hostile conduct

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block contributors for violations.
